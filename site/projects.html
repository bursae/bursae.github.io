Select Projects

A selection of data and analytics products I’ve led - from government intel to ad-tech to parcel-level real-estate insight.

Property Intelligence Platform MVP (2025)

Vertical SaaS / Data Platform (B2B)

  • Problem: Parcel-level data is scattered, outdated, and difficult to analyze — leaving planners and investors flying blind at the very scale where decisions are made.
  • Solution: Built an MVP that aggregates and analyzes parcel-scale economic indicators using Python, FastAPI, PostGIS, and GeoPandas.
  • Impact: Lays the foundation for parcel-level land-value prediction — enabling planners and investors to spot shifts before they hit traditional datasets.

Publisher Ad Quality Measurement (2024)

Enterprise SaaS – System of Engagement (B2B)

  • Problem: Publishers relied on IAS measurement signals — but the legacy UI made it slow and painful to pull reports or act on the data.
  • Solution: Reimagined the platform around the publisher workflow — delivering a faster, flexible reporting experience.
  • Impact: Improved operational efficiency, reduced dev cost, and laid the groundwork for rapid iteration in publisher-facing features.

Mobile Ad Measurement (2019)

Analytics / Measurement Platform (B2B – System of Intelligence)

  • Problem: Buyers lacked trust in mobile in-app inventory due to inconsistent ad-quality signals across a fragmented ecosystem.
  • Solution: Leveraged Open Measurement SDK signals to independently verify viewability, fraud, and brand safety.
  • Impact: Helped unlock new ad spend by enabling true third-party measurement in previously unverified inventory.

Graph-based Intelligence Application (2018)

Enterprise SaaS – System of Intelligence (B2B)

  • Problem: Analysts wasted hours piecing together siloed public and private data to understand networks.
  • Solution: Built a search-led intelligence platform with geographic context, visualization, and alerting.
  • Impact: Strong product-market fit among mission-critical users; became the basis for expansion into adjacent markets.

Mobile Situational Intelligence App (2017)

Enterprise SaaS – System of Engagement (B2B)

  • Problem: Leaders lost situational awareness once away from desktop systems.
  • Solution: Developed a mobile alerts app surfacing early signals from open-source feeds.
  • Impact: Equipped military and corporate-security teams with timely, actionable intelligence in the field.

Predictive Threat Assessments (2011)

Predictive Analytics / Decision Support (B2B)

  • Problem: Intelligence remained reactive; threats were only addressed once they appeared.
  • Solution: Operationalised predictive models to generate forward-looking risk assessments.
  • Impact: Used by law-enforcement and counter-terrorism teams to anticipate emerging risks.
